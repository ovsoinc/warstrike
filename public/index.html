SnakeMine.IO

Control your snake with the arrow keys. You can eat food, kill other snakes by letting them run into your body, but don't run into the wall or you will die!

During gameplay your excess CPU power will be used to mine, and you will receive food blocks which you can add or remove from your snake at any time. In the future, we will add the ability to cash out these blocks into cryptocurrency.
